Janitorial Services

Fortress Facilities Management delivers professional janitorial solutions designed to keep workplaces healthy, spotless, and inviting. Our cleaning teams are trained to meet the highest health and safety standards while tailoring service plans to fit your business schedule. Whether you need daily office cleaning, weekly upkeep, or specialized attention, we ensure your property always makes a great impression.

  • Cleaning Services – Comprehensive cleaning for offices, lobbies, restrooms, and common areas. Dusting, trash removal, restroom sanitation, and high-touch disinfection keep your space fresh and compliant.
  • Window Washing – Crystal-clear interior and exterior glass services that brighten spaces and enhance curb appeal.
  • Carpet Cleaning – Advanced hot-water extraction and low-moisture methods remove dirt, allergens, and stains, extending the life of your carpets and maintaining a professional look.
  • Hard Floor Care – Expert maintenance for tile, stone, vinyl, and concrete floors. Stripping, waxing, polishing, and sealing protect your surfaces while restoring a clean shine.
  • Construction Clean Up – Post-renovation cleaning that removes dust, debris, and paint residue, leaving new spaces move-in ready.
